Advisory Report and Responsible Investment Assessment

July 11th, 2025

This document was drafted during Spring semester 2025 by the ESG Working Group. While the group had no official authority--that is, it could not enforce a investment policy--it was tasked with creating an advisory document to be sent to the Board of Regents, who then get to decide whether or not to adopt the policy. This document closely follows the Episcopal Church No-Buy List but incorporates more policies, including channels for disclosure and petitions. This committee was spurred on by student protestors, and we hope it is adopted!
