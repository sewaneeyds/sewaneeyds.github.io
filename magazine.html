SYDS Magazine

The first edition of the SYDS magazine will be accepting submissions until Thanksgiving break! We hope to have our first printed copies out by reading day, so e-mail all submissions to grahaec0@sewanee.edu.

For our first edition, we are accepting any submissions of the following topics:

  • Visual Art
  • Short essays
  • Creative writing
  • Politics
  • Campus commentary

Any written pieces should be no more than 750 words, but preferably around 250-300 words. We might reject a submission if the piece doesn't align with SYDS, but we will first reach out and try to work through edits! If you have any questions, e-mail grahaec0@sewanee.edu or come to a SYDS Magazine meeting by signing up for our newsletter at the bottom of this page.
