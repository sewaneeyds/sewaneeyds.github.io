Introduction

The Sewanee Young Democratic Socialists (SYDS) is a left-wing group on Sewanee: The University of the South's campus. SYDS is committed to empowering students and advocating for equity on campus. Part of this mission involves forming a diverse community of students through projects like the SYDS Magazine, which is a (how often?) publication of student writing, art, and whatever else fits into a magazine. SYDS was once associated with YDSA, but isn't anymore. Instead, while SYDS is democratic-socialist by name, we welcome all left-wing schools of thought. We meet weekly on *where do we meet and when*, please stop by!

At this website, you can find everything SYDS related, which includes
  • Publications from our amazing contributors
  • Artwork from our amazing artists!
  • Important updates
  • Information on SYDS

Feel free to explore, and we hope to see you soon!


Why the simple website?

Website-making services have a few downsides.

  1. They cost money.
  2. If we miss a payment, we could lose the website.
  3. We wouldn't have full control in case something goes wrong.
  4. Web design is fun!

So, this website is maintained by hand using limited website coding knowledge. If anything acts up, please let us know at sewaneeydsa@gmail.com.
